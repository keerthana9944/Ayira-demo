Personalized Recommendations

Tailored advice based on your symptoms and health data

Increase Fiber Intake

Add more high-fiber foods like vegetables, fruits, and whole grains to help manage blood sugar levels and improve insulin sensitivity.

Stay Hydrated

Drink at least 8 glasses of water daily to help flush toxins and maintain proper metabolic function.

no_food

Limit Processed Foods

Reduce intake of processed foods, refined carbohydrates, and added sugars to help manage insulin resistance.

egg_alt

Include Lean Proteins

Incorporate lean protein sources like chicken, fish, tofu, and legumes to help stabilize blood sugar levels.

directions_run

Cardiovascular Exercise

Aim for 150 minutes of moderate-intensity cardio per week to improve insulin sensitivity and help with weight management.

fitness_center

Strength Training

Include 2-3 strength training sessions per week to build muscle mass, which can help improve insulin sensitivity.

self_improvement

Yoga and Stretching

Practice yoga or stretching exercises regularly to reduce stress levels, which can help balance hormones.

Prioritize Sleep

Aim for 7-9 hours of quality sleep per night to help regulate hormones and improve overall health.

Stress Management

Practice stress-reducing activities like meditation, deep breathing, or mindfulness to help balance cortisol levels.

Maintain Healthy Weight

Even a 5-10% weight reduction can significantly improve PCOS symptoms and hormonal balance.

Medication Options

Discuss with your doctor about medications like metformin for insulin resistance or birth control pills for hormone regulation.
